3 Factors That Spike Your Quote

Two offices with the exact same square footage can have very different prices. Here is why:

  • "Wet" Density (Restrooms & Kitchens): A 2,000 sq ft warehouse with 1 bathroom is cheap. A 2,000 sq ft office with 4 bathrooms and a large breakroom costs 30% more. Plumbing fixtures take the most time to sanitize.
  • Floor Type: Carpet is fast (vacuuming covers ground quickly). Tile requires sweeping, mopping, and occasional buffing, which is more labor-intensive.
  • Frequency: Increasing from "Weekly" to "Daily" does not multiply your price by 5. It usually triples it. You get a volume discount on frequency.

Special Section: Medical Office Costs

If you run a clinic, dental practice, or urgent care in areas like Cypress Creek or The Woodlands, expect to pay a 15-20% premium over standard office rates.

Why does medical cost more?

  • Compliance: Staff must be trained in OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens.
  • Chemicals: Hospital-grade disinfectants cost more than general cleaners.
  • Liability: Insurance premiums are higher for medical environments.

Pricing FAQ

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

Many franchises require a 1-year contract. Local providers (like Kassandra's) often offer month-to-month agreements because we believe our quality should keep you, not a signature.

Does the price include supplies?

Yes. A professional quote should always include cleaning solutions, rags, mops, and vacuums. You usually only provide the "consumables" (toilet paper, paper towels, hand soap, and trash liners).
