Houston’s 5 Most Overlooked Office Cleaning Tasks

And the Germs You Can’t See

Think your office is clean? Think again. Even with a regular cleaning schedule, there are common spots in every Houston workplace that rarely get the attention they need—yet they’re hotspots for germs.

If your cleaning service isn’t addressing these, you could be unknowingly risking staff health and client trust. Here’s what many forget—but Kassandra’s Cleaning Service never misses.

1. Keyboard & Mouse Disinfection

Keyboards can carry more bacteria than a toilet seat—especially in shared or front-desk setups. They're constantly touched but rarely disinfected properly.

2. Fridge Handles & Breakroom Buttons

Microwave keypads, fridge handles, and coffee makers are touched daily by multiple people but almost never sanitized. It’s the perfect breeding ground for bacteria and mold.

3. Door Handles & Light Switches

High-touch points like conference room door knobs and shared light switches get handled often—but skipped by general wipe-downs.

4. Chair Armrests

Especially in waiting rooms or shared offices, chair arms absorb skin oil, sweat, and germs. Yet most cleaners overlook them entirely.

5. Trash Can Lids & Bases

Emptying the trash isn’t enough. The lid and base are touched often and accumulate grime, odor, and hidden bacteria that can spread throughout the office.
